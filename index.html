Privacy Policy for LowStock

LowStock is a Shopify app developed by Bonis Tech that helps merchants receive email notifications when product inventory falls below a defined threshold.

Last updated: 29 December 2025
Policy overview

Data We Access

LowStock accesses the following store data:

  • Store domain
  • Product and variant inventory levels
  • Email addresses provided by the merchant for notifications

Data We Do NOT Collect

LowStock does not collect:

  • Customer personal data
  • Payment information
  • Customer orders
  • Browsing or tracking data

Email Notifications

Emails are sent only to email addresses configured by the merchant and are used solely for inventory alert notifications.

Data Retention

Store data is processed only to provide app functionality and is removed when the app is uninstalled.

Third-Party Services

LowStock uses third-party email delivery services (such as Resend) strictly to deliver notification emails.
